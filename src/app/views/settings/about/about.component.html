Requests Externalizer v{{globalVersion}}

This extension was made with Angular 15, and can be used to send requests or media to any program outside the browser.

For example, you can use it to send images directly to an image editor, or send a video stream to your video player.

The source code of the extension is available at:

https://github.com/Dokkaltek/requests-externalizer.git

Privacy

All data is stored client-side and never leaves the browser, so we will never see any dubious information you may be leaking.

This means that can feel reassured that your toilet schedule won't be sent to any third party business or any business at all from this extension.

Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to work correctly. Mainly the following:

  • Read your browsing history -> It's required to store the requests from background tabs so that if they weren't active their requests can appear in the list of the popup.
  • Communicate with third party applications -> I mean... how do you expect us to send things to third party applications without this?
